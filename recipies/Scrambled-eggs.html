Scrambled Eggs

Description

This recipie will give you the information to create any consistency or kind of scrambled eggs that meets your needs. Exact directions do not need to be followed, edit the recipe as needed.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Desired Seasonings

Steps

  1. Add 3-4 whole eggs to a bowl and whisk until bubbles have formed on the surface.
  2. Add a pan to medium heat and add olive oil. Wait until pan has heated, and add eggs.
  3. Use a spatula to move the mixture in the pan until it has begun to solidify.
  4. Continue step 3 until eggs have reached the disired doneness, then remove from heat and add seasoning.
