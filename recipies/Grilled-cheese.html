Grilled Cheese

Description

This recipe will make a simple grilled cheese to any desired doneness.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of White Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium/high heat depending on desired doneness.
  2. Add butter to 1 side of each piece of bread.
  3. Place one piece of bread in the pan, add a piece of cheese and the other piece of bread on top.
  4. Check doneness of bottom piece every 30-60 seconds and flip when complete.
  5. Repeat step 3 until cheese has melted and bread has been cooked adequately.
