PB and J Sandwich

Description

This easy recipe makes 1 Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of white bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Evenly spread peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  2. Evenly spread jelly on the other piece.
  3. Put both pieces together, cut diagonally or in half.
